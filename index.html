Refund Policy

We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If you're not happy, our refund policy is designed to make returns and exchanges as simple as possible.

  • Eligibility: Items must be returned within 30 days of purchase, unused, and in their original packaging.
  • Process: To initiate a return, contact our support team. We will guide you through the process and arrange for a pickup or provide shipping instructions.
  • Refunds: Once the returned item is inspected and approved, a refund will be processed to your original payment method within 7-10 business days.
